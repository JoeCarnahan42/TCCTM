About Me:

My name is Joseph Carnahan, but I usually go by Joe. I am a machinist/mechanic turned software engineer. I have worked in the industrial field for 5 years, and I am now changing careers. My journey started in high school when I was first exposed to the trades. As a kid who knew nothing about what he wanted to do(but a lot about football), the small amount of schooling was appealing. After school, I ended up as an apprentice toolmaker, polishing die parts for more experienced toolmakers. Shortly after starting this position, covid-19 really took over the country and the company restructured and changed their priorities, causing toolmakers to be unnecessary. The company switched their main priority to automation, where I began learning how to build complex custom automated machinerey. Here you can find some of my builds! Currently, I am a freelance software engineer participating in the online bootcamp Parsity. Here, I am learning full-stack development and honing my coding skills as a software engineer. At the moment, I am currently seeking a role as a "Junior Software Engineer".
